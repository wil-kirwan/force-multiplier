## HighLevel publishing and distribution fields

Keep the editorial planning logic unchanged. Add HighLevel deployment fields when the user's publishing stack is HighLevel.

### Calendar fields to include
- destination blog site
- author
- category
- slug status
- linked funnel or website page
- CTA destination
- Social Planner publishing slot
- email campaign or email-template handoff
- workflow or nurture dependency

### Distribution default
When social or email distribution is part of the plan, prefer:
- HighLevel Social Planner for social scheduling
- HighLevel email campaigns or templates for newsletter handoff
- HighLevel workflows when the user wants follow-up tied to leads or bookings

Use published HighLevel URLs for freshness and decay checks when there is no local content repo.
