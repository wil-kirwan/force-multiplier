## HighLevel-first CRM sync for LinkedIn Ads

Replace generic CRM sync instructions with HighLevel by default.

### Default lead flow
LinkedIn Lead Gen Form or landing-page conversion -> HighLevel contact -> tags and custom fields -> opportunity creation -> pipeline routing -> owner assignment -> workflow follow-up

Use HighLevel for:
- contact creation and dedupe
- opportunity creation and pipeline stage routing
- real-time lead handling and task creation
- downstream metrics like booked calls, SQLs, opportunities, and revenue

### ABM rule
Demandbase, 6sense, and similar ABM platforms are optional enrichment tools. They are not the default CRM or follow-up stack.

### What to audit
- lead form fields map cleanly into HighLevel fields
- routing logic sends the contact into the correct pipeline and stage
- workflows fire immediately after form submission
- campaign ROI is judged by HighLevel pipeline and revenue outcomes, not CPL alone
