## HighLevel publishing handoff

Keep the platform-specific copy generation unchanged. Add HighLevel as the preferred publishing and delivery path when the user uses HighLevel.

### Preferred destinations
- Social Planner for social post scheduling
- Email campaigns or email templates for newsletter deployment
- HighLevel blog posts, site pages, funnels, calendars, or forms for CTA destinations

### HighLevel handoff packet
For each repurposed asset, include:
- destination channel
- recommended HighLevel destination URL
- campaign or tag metadata to carry through
- whether the asset should route into a form, calendar, or funnel
- any workflow follow-up the post should trigger

If the user wants live social publishing, resolve social account IDs through the shared HighLevel handler before creating the post.
