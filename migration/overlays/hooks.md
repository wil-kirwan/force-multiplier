## HighLevel CTA execution for comment-keyword campaigns

When a hook or script uses `Comment [WORD]`, HighLevel is the default follow-up engine.

- trigger on Instagram Comment(s) On A Post inside HighLevel
- reply in comments when needed
- use Instagram Interactive Messenger or Instagram DM to deliver the promised asset or next step
- tag contacts and store the keyword, topic, source post, and offer in HighLevel
- continue the sequence with HighLevel email, SMS, pipeline, or calendar workflows
