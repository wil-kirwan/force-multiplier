## HighLevel outcome layer for Microsoft Ads

Keep Microsoft Ads platform checks intact, including UET, Enhanced Conversions, native goals, import validation, and Copilot placement review.

Use HighLevel as the default source for:
- lead records after submit or call
- booked appointments and call outcomes
- opportunity creation, stage progression, and won revenue
- `msclkid`, UTMs, and campaign identifiers stored on the contact or opportunity

### What to verify
- Microsoft click IDs and source metadata are stored in HighLevel
- forms, calls, or calendars create the right contact and opportunity
- offline revenue and pipeline outcomes can be tied back to the original campaign
- Copilot and imported campaign traffic is judged by real CRM outcomes, not just platform-reported conversions
