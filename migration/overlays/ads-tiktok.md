## HighLevel capture and pipeline mapping for TikTok Ads

Keep TikTok Pixel, Events API, Spark Ads, Smart+, safe-zone rules, and TikTok Shop requirements unchanged.

Use HighLevel as the default system for:
- capturing `ttclid`, UTMs, and campaign metadata
- contact creation from landing pages or lead flows
- pipeline stage tracking, appointment outcomes, and revenue attribution
- follow-up workflows after form submit, DM, comment, or booking when the user's stack supports that path

### What to verify
- `ttclid` is stored in HighLevel fields
- leads flow into contacts and, when appropriate, opportunities
- TikTok traffic can be judged by booked calls, pipeline movement, and revenue in HighLevel
- Smart+ and creative tests are evaluated against downstream CRM quality, not only platform CPA
