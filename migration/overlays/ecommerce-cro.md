## HighLevel retention and lead-capture layer

Keep Shopify as the storefront when the store is already on Shopify. Use HighLevel as the default recovery, nurture, and CRM layer where possible.

### Default companion stack
- abandoned cart, browse abandonment, review requests, post-purchase nurture, and win-back flows run through HighLevel workflows when the data path exists
- email and SMS follow-up default to HighLevel
- lead capture, quiz funnels, consultation offers, and booking flows default to HighLevel forms, surveys, funnels, or calendars
- tags, custom fields, and opportunities track shopper segments and high-intent behaviors in HighLevel
