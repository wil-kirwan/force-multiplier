## HighLevel audience and conversion mapping for YouTube Ads

Keep Google Ads and YouTube measurement intact.

Use HighLevel for:
- customer-list and lifecycle-segment audiences when retargeting or Customer Match is part of the plan
- appointment bookings, qualified leads, pipeline stages, and closed revenue after the click
- CTA destinations that route into HighLevel calendars, forms, funnels, or site pages

### Practical rule
Judge YouTube lead-gen performance with both:
1. Google Ads view and click-through conversion data
2. HighLevel downstream outcomes such as booked calls, show rate, opportunity creation, and revenue

When these disagree, report the gap clearly.
